The runtime character and equipment GLBs are technical-slice derivatives of
KayKit CC0 assets by Kay Lousberg. They are not Taleforge-original art.

- KayKit Adventurers Free 2.0: <https://kaylousberg.itch.io/kaykit-adventurers>
- KayKit Skeletons Free 1.1: <https://kaylousberg.itch.io/kaykit-skeletons>
- KayKit Character Animations Free 1.1: <https://kaylousberg.itch.io/kaykit-character-animations>
- Licence: [CC0 1.0 Universal](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)
- Download date: 2026-07-12
- Archive hashes and byte sizes: `asset-records/characters/kaykit-source-provenance.json`

The normalized GLBs in this directory are generated runtime derivatives. The
original archives and editable source files remain in the ignored asset
workbench and are not served by the application.
